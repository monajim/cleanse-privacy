01

Overview

Cleanse ("we", "our", or "us") is a storage optimization and device management application for Android. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how the app operates on your device.

This Privacy Policy explains what information Cleanse accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information. By using Cleanse, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Core principle: Cleanse is designed to work entirely on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or sell your personal data to any third party.

02

Information We Collect

Cleanse accesses the following categories of information solely to provide its core functionality on your device:

  • Storage usage data — file sizes, directory paths, and cache locations — used to identify junk files and calculate storage savings.
  • Contact metadata — names, phone numbers, and email addresses — used solely within the app to identify duplicate or incomplete contacts for your review.
  • App usage information — installed app sizes and cache data — used to surface optimization opportunities.

All processing happens locally on your device. No personal information is uploaded to external servers.

03

Device Permissions

Cleanse may request the following Android permissions to deliver its features:

  • READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / MANAGE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — to scan and display storage usage information.
  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — to delete identified junk and cache files upon your explicit confirmation.
  • READ_CONTACTS / WRITE_CONTACTS — to analyze, display, and (with your approval) remove duplicate or incomplete contacts.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — (Pro plan only) to enable automatic background cleaning when your device starts.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — to run background cleaning tasks with a visible notification, as required by Android.
  • INTERNET — to process in-app subscription purchases via Google Play Billing.

You can revoke any permission at any time through your device's Settings → Apps → Cleanse → Permissions.

04

How We Use Information

Information accessed by Cleanse is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • To display storage analytics and identify files eligible for deletion.
  • To detect duplicate or low-quality contacts and present them for your review.
  • To execute cleaning operations you explicitly initiate or schedule.
  • To calculate and display storage space recovered.

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, analytics reporting, or any purpose beyond the in-app functionality described above.

05

Third-Party Services

Cleanse integrates with the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Play Billing — handles subscription and purchase transactions. We do not receive or store your payment details. See Google's privacy policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

No advertising SDKs, analytics frameworks, or data brokers are integrated into Cleanse. Your usage data is never shared with, sold to, or licensed by any third party.

06

Data Storage & Security

All data accessed by Cleanse remains on your device. We do not operate remote servers that store user data. Subscription status is managed securely by Google Play and verified through standard Play Billing APIs.

App preferences and settings are stored locally in Android's standard SharedPreferences storage, which is private to the app and inaccessible to other applications.

07

Children's Privacy

Cleanse is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the app in a way that raises privacy concerns, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

08

Your Rights

Since Cleanse does not transmit or store your data on external servers, there is no user data held by us to access, export, or delete remotely.

You may exercise control over your data directly on your device:

  • Revoke permissions via Android Settings at any time.
  • Uninstalling Cleanse removes all locally stored app preferences immediately.
  • Contact Google Play to manage or cancel subscription billing information.

For significant changes, we will provide notice within the app.
